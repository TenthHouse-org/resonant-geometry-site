How we work

AI-accelerated. Expert-reviewed. Honestly delivered.

1 — Scope

A short call or written brief pins down exactly what you need, the standard it must meet, and the date you need it by. Fixed quote before work begins.

2 — Research & draft

AI-assisted research and drafting runs around the clock, cross-checked in multiple independent passes — so a week of desk work compresses into days without skipping the verification.

3 — Review & deliver

Every deliverable gets a final expert review before it ships — by the founder (B.S., Mechanical Engineering), against GD&T and documentation standards, with a source list you can audit.

We tell you what the evidence supports, what it doesn't, and when the honest answer is "it depends." If a job needs a licensed professional — a PE stamp, a legal opinion, a clinical judgment — we say so and help you hand off cleanly.

About

A small practice with an unusual engine

Resonant Geometry LLC is a New Hampshire technical design and research practice founded by Robert Brown (B.S., Mechanical Engineering) — CAD and product design (SolidWorks, AutoCAD), GD&T to ASME Y14.5, finite-element analysis, and documentation built to be read, not just filed.

What makes the practice unusual is the engine behind it: design judgment paired with AI research systems that work around the clock, with every output cross-checked and expert-reviewed before it reaches you. Small-firm attention, larger-firm throughput.

Resonant Geometry works alongside Tenth House, a New Hampshire nonprofit research organization; our commercial work helps sustain its research.
